SOME IMPORTANT WEBISITES OF THE UNIVERSITY OF GHANA

  • Main University Website

    • This is the official website of the University of Ghana, where you can find information about the university's history, academic programs, admission requirements, research, events, and news.

  • SAKAI

    • The Sakai website is an online learning management system used by the University of Ghana to support teaching and learning activities. The website provides students with access to course materials, such as syllabi, assignments, readings, and multimedia resources, as well as communication tools, such as discussion forums, email, and chat. Sakai is an essential website for students to access and manage their course materials and communicate with their instructors and classmates.

  • MIS Web

    • The Management Information System (MIS) Web is a website that provides administrative services to students, faculty, and staff. This website allows students to register for courses, pay tuition fees, check their academic records, and apply for graduation. It also allows faculty and staff to access information related to their employment, such as salary information, leave balances, and benefits. The MIS Web is essential for students, faculty, and staff to manage their administrative affairs at the university.

  • STS

    • The Students' Technology Services (STS) website provides technical support and services to students at the University of Ghana. The website provides resources and assistance for computer and software issues, internet connectivity, and mobile devices. It also provides access to software and hardware for academic purposes. The STS website is essential for students to access technical support and resources that are necessary for their academic work.
